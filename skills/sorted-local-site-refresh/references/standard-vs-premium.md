# Standard vs Premium

## Standard build

Purpose:
Deliver a fast, clean, trustworthy local-business website closely aligned to the approved mockup.

Characteristics:
- faithful layout
- practical responsiveness
- clean typography
- restrained transitions
- straightforward sections
- quick implementation
- strong CTA clarity
- polished but commercially safe

Motion guidance:
- hover states
- smooth scrolling
- subtle reveal timing
- restrained transitions

Best for:
- fast delivery
- lower budgets
- practical refreshes
- mockup implementation

## Premium build

Purpose:
Deliver a stronger visual experience while preserving the same business identity and conversion structure.

Characteristics:
- stronger hero composition
- richer typography hierarchy
- refined spacing rhythm
- more cinematic imagery
- enhanced transitions and motion
- layered visual depth
- more memorable sections
- stronger visual storytelling

Motion guidance:
- staggered reveals
- refined hover systems
- subtle parallax
- animated hero composition
- section choreography
- smooth transitions between content groups

Premium rules:
- do not redesign the business into a startup
- do not overload with effects
- do not reduce clarity for aesthetics
- preserve practical usability
- preserve trust and readability
- effects should support the business, not distract from it

A good Premium build should feel:
- more expensive
- more polished
- more intentional
- more memorable

without feeling:
- corporate
- futuristic
- experimental for the sake of it
- overdesigned
