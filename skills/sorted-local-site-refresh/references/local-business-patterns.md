# Local Business Page Patterns

## Martial arts / gym / fitness

Primary goals:
- book intro
- view timetable
- build trust
- show community

Recommended structure:
1. Hero with class imagery and direct CTA
2. Program or class cards
3. Benefits and philosophy
4. Kids or beginner section
5. Facility or coach credibility
6. Testimonials
7. Timetable or booking CTA
8. Location/contact/footer

Visual direction:
- energetic but clean
- strong photography
- community-driven
- confident typography
- bold CTA contrast
- avoid over-tech visuals

## Trade / repair / specialist service

Primary goals:
- immediate trust
- phone calls
- clear process
- product or service clarity

Recommended structure:
1. Hero with clear service statement
2. Trust bar: warranty, turnaround, delivery, support
3. Service or product navigation cards
4. Social proof or latest work
5. Product or service grid
6. Why choose us
7. Supported brands/categories
8. Contact CTA
9. Footer

Visual direction:
- industrial but polished
- darker palettes acceptable
- practical layout
- clear product imagery
- obvious contact paths
- avoid fake startup visuals

## Beauty / personal care

Primary goals:
- bookings
- trust
- atmosphere
- visual appeal

Recommended structure:
1. Elegant hero
2. Services grid
3. Featured treatments
4. Social proof/gallery
5. Pricing or package section
6. About/studio section
7. Booking CTA
8. Contact/footer

Visual direction:
- softer motion
- lighter spacing
- premium editorial feel
- strong imagery
- calm typography

## Professional services

Primary goals:
- credibility
- trust
- consultation requests

Recommended structure:
1. Hero with concise value statement
2. Services
3. Process or methodology
4. Results or proof
5. About/team
6. Testimonials
7. Consultation CTA
8. Footer

Visual direction:
- restrained
- clean spacing
- minimal motion
- highly readable
- no gimmicks

## Ecommerce/service hybrid

Primary goals:
- trust
- product discovery
- support confidence
- conversions

Recommended structure:
1. Hero
2. Trust bar
3. Shop/service entry blocks
4. Featured products
5. Social proof
6. Why choose us
7. Categories or supported brands
8. Help/contact CTA
9. Footer

Visual direction:
- product-first
- clean grids
- practical UX
- visible pricing
- clear CTAs
- mobile-safe layouts
